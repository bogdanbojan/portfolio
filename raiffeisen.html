After understanding Lean UX and Agile workflow principles, I tried to make use of all that knowledge and create a mock app. A finance related project seemed most interesting since I frequently use those type of apps and can bring my own take to it.

In the making of this app, I tried to stay true to the brands identity. That meant maintaining a clear chromatic structure that is embedded in Raiffeisens personality, using personas, user maps and wireframes to get a clearer view of what and how i should approach the UX/UI design.

After that, I made sure to keep a modern touch in the overall design. That included shape language and designing additional custom elements such as the cards or various interactions.

Designing for a mobile-centric audience was key. Finance apps tend to be used quite a lot nowadays from our phones in order to pay for things in an easy fashion or check our balance/make various transactions. I used that to my advantage and figured out what elements I should keep from an information architecture point of view. Then I went ahead and added all that research into these final mock-ups.
