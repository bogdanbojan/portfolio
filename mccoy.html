Concert App

Product Designer

A pop-up website for the launch of a timeless album. My love for jazz and UX combined!

I love jazz so I figured I might as well do a website around it. This is a concert launch website. Basically I thought of how would we promote McCoy Tyner's album if he were to release it today. Blue Note had amazing cover art for its musicians. Reid Miles, the guy who did most of the album art for the label's artists is well known for the typography and interesting compositions that he created. It always is very inspiring when you look at what Reid made.

Therefore, I thought of doing something similar. A pop-up website that promotes an album and also gives you the chance to buy a ticket to the first hearing of the tracks. I made a couple of cover re-interpretations of famous blue note records. After that, I chose one and made the website around it.

It was important for me to approach this project in a creative manner. From the premise of the whole website to the alignment, use of typography to guide the user and provide a visual grid to the whole page- every element has a creative factor while also maintaining the functional aspect of the user experience. It's a mirroring of the whole bebop period in the design.
